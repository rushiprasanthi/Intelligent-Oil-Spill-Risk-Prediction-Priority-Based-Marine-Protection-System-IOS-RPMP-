# IOS-RPMP Demo Walkthrough Script

## 1. Introduction (30 seconds)
"This system supports early decision-making during marine oil spills.
It prioritizes response actions based on spill severity, environmental exposure,
and response logistics."

---

## 2. Live Interaction (1 minute)
- Right-click on the map to simulate a spill
- Show that different locations produce different priorities
- Highlight nearest port and ETA changes

---

## 3. Explain the Intelligence (1 minute)
"Our model separates baseline hazard from ecological impact.
Large spills under strong wind already represent risk,
even before sensitive zones are reached."

---

## 4. Priority Escalation
- P3: Monitoring (low risk)
- P2: Secondary Defense (moderate hazard)
- P1: Immediate Protection (high exposure & sensitivity)

---

## 5. Transparency Statement
"This is not a black-box AI.
All decisions are explainable and based on environmental logic."

---

## 6. Closing
"The system is lightweight, explainable, and extensible to AI or satellite data
in future phases."
